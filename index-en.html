Services

Web development

A website is the way a business, person, brand present nowadays to the world. Often the website is accessed from a mobile device. That's why is important that the pages are responsive and showcase the best content. My approach is to meet the client, understand his/her needs and propose the layout with the best price/quality option.

Prices: it depends. A small presentation site with 4-5 pages (ideal for small business, personal portfolio, like this website) about $100 per page. A larger website that require a database, blog, content media from $800 up to $3000. Write me for a quote then we can talk about it.

Web maintenance

Websites are "live animals" that evolve overtime. With fast technology changes they often need maintenace in order to upgrade the appearance and the content. To reach this goal I can redisign an existing website to give a more modern and good looking appearance. Also update the content and setup your social media accounts.
Prices: $50/hour • $300/month

Video promo
