About

Hey, I'm Nicodemo. I develop, build, create websites.

After my master degree in computer science I worked for a couple of years in Padua (Italy) as back-end developer (Java, PHP, SQL). Then I decided to go to live in Australia, where I got a sponsorship as full stack developer (.net, C#). Four years later (and the Australian citizenship) I had the opportunity to follow a sport project adventure that let me travel the world. In this context I had new doors opened that gave me the chance to work in the tourist and hospitality industries.

My constant desire to learn something new every day brought me back to the tech world. I decided to update my skills by learning last years trends and how to use new platforms, languages and web development tools.

My mission: create clean, simple and user friendly websites. That at the same time give a great impression to the visitor.
